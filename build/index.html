Edinburgh Malaysian Students' Association

About Us

We are the Edinburgh Malaysian Students' Association (EMSA) we strive to provide a home for Malaysian students in Edinburgh.

Developed from a collaboration of Malaysian students from the University of Edinburgh, Heriot Watt University, Queen Margaret University and Edinburgh Napier University, we are the largest Malaysian student organisation in Scotland.

Our visions and missions can be found in our constitution.
